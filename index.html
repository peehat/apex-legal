APEX Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

APEX ("the app," "we," "us") is a social app for car enthusiasts — track your drives, share your build, connect with other drivers. This page explains what information APEX collects, why, and how you can control or delete it.

Information we collect

To run your account and the app's features, APEX stores information you provide directly or that the app collects with your permission:

  • Account info: email address, username, display name, password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
  • Profile content: bio, avatar photo, home base/location text you enter
  • Vehicles & builds: car details, mods, build log entries, photos you upload
  • Location: with your permission, used to map and record drives you choose to log, show nearby spots/meets/other drivers, and place your posts on the map. Location is only collected while the relevant feature is in use, or in the background specifically while you are actively recording a drive you started. You can deny or revoke location access at any time in your device settings — most of the app still works without it.
  • Posts & social activity: feed posts, comments, honks (likes), photos, drive routes and stats you choose to share
  • Photos: images you upload for your profile, cars, build log, or posts. Photos you upload have location/device metadata (EXIF) automatically stripped before storage.

How we use this information

We use your information solely to operate APEX's features: showing your profile and posts to other users, mapping your drives, powering the live/social map, and maintaining your account. We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers, and we do not use your data for third-party advertising.

Where your data is stored

APEX stores data using Supabase, a third-party hosting and database provider, and standard cloud photo/file storage. Your data is subject to that provider's security practices and to applicable law. We take reasonable steps to secure your data, but no system is 100% secure.

What other users can see

Content you choose to post publicly (feed posts, public drives, garage builds set to public visibility) is visible to other users of the app. You control visibility settings for your garage, drives, and posts in-app, including friends-only and private options, an anonymous posting mode, and the ability to hide your live location ("ghost mode").

Your rights & control over your data

  • You can edit or delete individual posts, photos, cars, and drives at any time within the app.
  • You can permanently delete your entire account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account inside the app. This action is irreversible and removes your profile, cars, posts, drives, comments, and other associated data.
  • You can revoke camera, photo library, or location permissions at any time in your device's system settings.

Children's privacy

APEX is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as APEX's features evolve. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated "Last updated" date, and significant changes may prompt re-acceptance of terms in-app.
